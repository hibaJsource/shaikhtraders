Top Questions

( 1 ) What safety measures has Daraz taken to make sure packages are not infected?

What safety measures has Daraz taken to make sure packages are not infected?
Our DEX heroes - delivery agents and operations team have been instructed to wear protective gear, including gloves and masks. Our Logistics Partners have been given the same instructions. We are also making sure that packages are being disinfected at customers' doorstep wherever possible.

( 2 ) Will there be delays in delivery due to COVID -19?

Will there be delays in delivery due to COVID -19?
Due to the government’s recent COVID-19 restrictions on transportation services, we have resumed the delivery for essential items like health and cleaning products as well as household grocery items in Sindh, while we are delivering non-essential items across the regions. All customers should expect a delay in receiving their orders. Customers will be notified about their orders separately through email and SMS. Our priority remains to ensure the safety of our customers, sellers, and employees.

( 3 ) Can the COVID-19 virus be transmitted via packages?

Can the COVID-19 virus be transmitted via packages?
There is no evidence indicating packages or their contents carry any risks. We are monitoring the situation and will cooperate with relevant authorities. We are following WHO’s guidelines for COVID-19 closely.

( 5 ) How do I return my order?

How do I return my order?
If you are not 100% satisfied with your purchase from Shaikh Traders, you can return your item(s) for a full refund within 7 days of purchase and 14 days. Please follow the written steps below.
Step 1- Click Account and click View All in the My Order section.
Step 2- Select the order you wish to return and click on the Return button.
Step 3- Fill in the Return Form. The available return options [pick-up/drop-off] will be displayed in this form.
Step 4- Pack your parcel securely, with the product in the original and undamaged manufacturer's packaging along with any accessories, manuals, free items, etc. as delivered.
Step 5- Print the Return Label by visiting going into your returns page through desktop. Or write in block letters the Return Tracking Number on paper and secure the paper or printed Return Label on top of the return package. If the drop off location is selected as Shaikh Traders Shop then please pack the item and write Order number and RA number and drop it off to the nearest Shaikh Traders Shop
Note: It is no longer necessary to print out the Return Label. Customers can now authorize returns with the Return Tracking Number that is issued after completing the steps above.

( 6 ) What is our refund policy?

What is our refund policy?
The Voucher discount code can only be applied once. The leftover amount will not be refunded or used for next purchase even if the value of order is smaller than voucher value.
