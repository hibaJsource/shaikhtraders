Top Questions

( 1 ) If my order is cancelled, will I get my Collectible Vouchers back with my refunds?

If my order is cancelled, will I get my Collectible Vouchers back with my refunds?
Yes, if you place a order using a collectible voucher and the order is returned or cancelled, the voucher will be returned to your wallet. However, the voucher validity will be the same as original voucher so you can only use the voucher if its still valid and we are trying our best to process the return as soon as possible. Please stay at home and be safe.

( 3 ) What is ‘Shake Shake’?

What is ‘Shake Shake’?
Customers on the Shaikh app have the chance to win Shaikh or brand specific vouchers by shaking their phones at certain times during Campaign.

( 4 ) Can I use more than one collectible vouchers in my order?

Can I use more than one collectible vouchers in my order?
Yes, you can use multiple collectible vouchers in one order. The limit for voucher use in one order is a maximum of one Daraz voucher per order and a maximum of one seller voucher per seller.
Furthermore, you can use different collectible vouchers from different sellers in one order. However, the limit is of one voucher per seller.

( 5 ) My order value is more than my voucher amount. What do I do?

My order value is more than my voucher amount. What do I do?
If your voucher value is less than the order amount, you’ll be asked to select an additional payment method to complete the payment.
You can select the payment option at the time of checkout.

( 6 ) Why am I unable to collect vouchers?

Why am I unable to collect vouchers?
Collectible Vouchers are of a different kind like some are for the New customer and some for the Returning customer. If you are an old customer and collecting a voucher of New Customer then you won't be able to collect it and if you are and regular customer and collecting the voucher of returning customer then again you won't be able to collect it.
Whenever you face such issue please do check are you meeting the eligibility criteria of that voucher if not then you won't be able to collect it.
