( 1 ) Can I return oversea Products?

Can I return oversea Products?
The following return policy applies to overseas products and supersedes all other terms and conditions of sales on Daraz and the Daraz return policy:
Returns for the global items are only accepted for the reason listed below:
Item is defective/ Item is damaged upon delivery or is not according to the sellers specification mentioned on the website,
Item is Wrong: Item delivered is not the item you ordered.
Note: Returns on any other basis will not be accepted.
Feel free to contact us via Live Chat if you have any confusion.
NOTE:As Lock-down situation due to COVID-19 situation in the country. You might experience delays in the return timelines. We apologize for the inconvenience and we are trying our best to process the return as soon as possible. Please stay at home and be safe.

( 2 ) How do I know if my product has a warranty?

How do I know if my product has a warranty?
If a warranty is offered on a product, the warranty period will be displayed on the product page, to the right of the product image below “Delivery”.
If a product is sold by multiple vendors, the warranty period offered by each vendor will be displayed to the right of “Seller” in the “Sold by x more sellers” box below the product image.

( 3 ) How do I return my order?

How do I return my order?
If you are not 100% satisfied with your purchase from Daraz, you can return your item(s) for a full refund within 7 days of purchase and 14 days for Shaikh Traders item.
Note: The refund timeline will be based on payment method.

( 4 ) What are the mandatory information and steps to return a product?

What are the mandatory information and steps to return a product?
To ensure that your return is accepted and processed smoothly please follow the below steps;
1. Pack item(s) and include the following documents.
- Manual/User guide
- Order Invoice
- Complete accessories with freebies.

2. Print return label and paste it on the box, Print label can be printed after you have finished filling up your Online Returns Form.
