Top Questions

( 1 ) How do I join Shaikh Store?

How do I join Shaikh Store?
Currently, participation in Shaikh Store is on an "invite-only" basis, at the discretion of the Shaikh team based on performance. Reach out to your Key Account Manager to learn more about enlisting.

( 2 ) What is a Flagship Store?

What is a Flagship Store?
Flagship stores are a selection of leading international and local brands, online brands and retailers. Flagship stores are the cornerstone and core brands that make up Shaikh Store.
Remember that Shaikh Traders has "Online Flagship Stores". They are not physical outlets/ stores.

( 3 ) How can I identify a Shaikh Traders product in the website?

How can I identify a Shaikh Traders product in the website?
You can identify "Shaikh Traders" products by the "Shaikh Traders" badge on the product's page and catalog page for specific products. These products are sold from the Shaikh Traders Flagship Stores and there are certified & trusted Shaikh sellers.

( 4 ) What do I do if my order did not reach in the promised lead time?

What do I do if my order did not reach in the promised lead time?
If you do not receive your Shaikh Store orders within the expected delivery dates specified for your order, you can first check the status of your order in the "My Orders" page in your Daraz account. This allows you to see if the seller has shipped the order yet or not.

( 5 ) What is Shaikh return policy?

What is Shaikh return policy?
Shaikh Traders offers 14-days easy returns, allowing you to return the product within 14 days after delivery. As per Shaikh return policy, you need to fill in the Online Return Form through your Shaikh Account.

( 6 ) What is Shaikh Store?

What is Shaikh Store?
DarazMall is basically Pakistan's largest shopping mall! It is a dedicated channel on the Daraz platform with a carefully curated selection of branded products sourced directly from brands and brand authorized sellers.
DarazMall offers customers the highest-quality online shopping experience by delivering on the following promises:
1. 100% Authenticity: On Shaikh Store, we guarantee you will receive only original, authentic branded products.
2. 14 day easy returns: 14 day easy returns for all Shaikh Store products, except non-returnable categories as per Shaikh guidelines. 3. Shaikh Express Delivery
