Top Questions

( 1 ) What is the total wallet balance?

What is the total wallet balance?
Your total Wallet balance is a combination of your "Top-Up"amount, your "Bonuses" & your "Refund Amount".
Furthermore, you can view your total available balance on the main page of your wallet.

( 2 ) What is Shaikh Wallet Bonus?

What is Shaikh Wallet Bonus?
Shaikh Wallet Bonus is your special reward provided to you in your Wallet balance. Furthermore, bonus amount cannot be withdrawn.
Note: You may earn bonus based on different on-going promotion on Daraz. Keep an eye out for exciting bonus earning opportunities throughout the year.
Everybody gets Lucky!

( 3 ) How to pay through Shaikh Wallet?

How to pay through Shaikh Wallet?
It's easy! Follow the steps below:
1. Once you have activated and have sufficient funds in your Shaikh wallet
2. Add a product to your cart
3. Select"Shaikh Wallet"as your payment method.

*If you have insufficient funds in the wallet, don't worry! You can top up during checkout to make up for the full amount in the cart.

( 4 ) What payment methods can be used to top-up Shaikh wallet?

What payment methods can be used to top-up Shaikh wallet?
You can Top Up your Daraz Wallet using the below shared payment options:

1. Debit / Credit Card(Visa / Master)
2. Easypaisa Wallet(Mobile Account)
3. JazzCash Wallet(Mobile Account)
4. Internet Banking
•5. HBL direct transfer

( 5 ) How to withdraw refunds from the wallet?

How to withdraw refunds from the wallet?
1. Click on"Details"on the main wallet page.
2. Click on"Withdraw Refunds"in the refunds section.
3. Select your bank and enter your account details where you would like your refund to be deposited.
4. Verify your details through email and confirm withdrawal.
5. Once you submit your withdrawal request, your refund balance will be frozen in your wallet, and you will not be able to make any purchases with it.

( 6 ) How can you activate the Shaikh wallet?

How can you activate the Shaikh wallet?
Shaikh Wallet is just like your Wallet in your pocket. You may use it easily for shopping with us without any hidden charges.
It’s very easy to activate the Daraz Wallet. Follow the below steps:

1. Login your"Daraz"Account.
2. Tap on"Wallet"icon available on the top-right corner of the Daraz App
3. Click on"Activate"
4. Verify your phone number by entering the SMS code sent to your registered device.

Congratulation! Wallet Activated.
