Top Questions

( 1 ) I ordered multiple items but I have received only one item so far. What’s going on?

I ordered multiple items but I have received only one item so far. What’s going on?
Items sourced from different sellers are often shipped separately to make sure that there is no delay in fulfillment of your order. Rest assured that you will get all your items within the delivery period mentioned on the product page of those items.
Delivery charges confirmed at the time of order placement are spread throughout your order.

( 2 ) Can I change my shipping address after I have placed my order?

Can I change my shipping address after I have placed my order?
Unfortunately, your shipping details cannot be changed after your order has been placed.
However, you may cancel the order and re-order with right address.

( 3 ) My parcel has been reported missing. What now?

My parcel has been reported missing. What now?
No need to worry. If you have any such concerns, you may contact us through Live Chat for a possible resolution. We've got you covered!

( 4 ) Why are my items shipped separately?

Why are my items shipped separately?
Items sourced from different sellers are often shipped separately to make sure that there is no delay in fulfillment of your order. Rest assured that you will get all your items within the delivery period mentioned on the product pages of those items. There are no hidden charges on partial shipment.

( 5 ) Does Shaikh Traders deliver orders outside Pakistan?

Does Shaikh Traders deliver orders outside Pakistan?
No! Shaikh Traders does not deliver orders outside Pakistan.

( 6 ) I found the package open and the product seal broken on delivery. What should I do?

I found the package open and the product seal broken on delivery. What should I do?
You are advised to not receive such order. However, if you do receive any such package which is open, severely damaged or tampered, please get in touch with us immediately via LIVE CHAT.
